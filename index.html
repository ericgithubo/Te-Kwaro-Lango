Welcome to Tekwaro Lango,

We are a vibrant and rich cultural heritage of the Lango people from Uganda. Lango culture is a tapestry of traditions, customs, and beliefs that have been woven together over centuries. It is a culture that values community, respect, and hospitality, and is expressed through vibrant music, dance, art, and storytelling.
